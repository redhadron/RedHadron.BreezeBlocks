Da = Depth amount, Db = depth binary, Dc = Depth centered amount.

universal format of numbers:
  [num"p"][num"n"][num"d"]
  at least one section must be used.
  p is for pixels
  n is for numerator
  d is for denominator
  when denominator is used without numerator, numerator is 1.
  when numerator is used without denominator, denominator is the smallest power of 10 greater than the numerator.
  examples:
    1p = 1
    1p2n = 1.2
    1p2n3d = 1.66
    2n3d = 0.66
    3d = 0.33
    1p3d = 1.33
    
Thickness specifiers:
  T = frame and big details and little details
  F = frame
  D = big details and little details
  B = big details
  L = little details

version format:
  Major.minor.patch
    patch = nobody should have to care about this change, not even makers of other mods that link to assets of this mod. no assets, translations, or textures are added or removed. Can downgrade without affecting other mods that link to this mod.
    minor = may add things, but must not add game assets (blocks or items) or block states. downgrading does not result in invalid blocks or inventory items in the world.
    major = may add game assets or block states. downgrading may cause some blocks or inventory items in the world to become invalid.
    I don't plan to make any changes that are bigger than major (an example would be a change that removed a block ID), but if the need arose, a new supermajor version would be added: supermajor.major.minor.patch.
