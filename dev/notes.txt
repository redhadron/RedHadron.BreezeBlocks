useless tags (just rename the shape):
  Ud = upside-down
  Iv = inverted
  Np = non-prismatic

  Bw = basketweave
potential tags:
  Is = inset
  Fl = frameless
  for both of the above tags:
    can come before the shape name(s) to affect only the outer frame
    can come after any shape name to affect the shape
    Can come after an underscore after the shape names to affect every shape individually

Da = Depth amount, Db = depth binary, Dc = Depth centered amount.

universal format of numbers:
  [num"p"][num"n"][num"d"]
  at least one section must be used.
  p is for pixels
  n is for numerator
  d is for denominator
  when denominator is used without numerator, numerator is 1.
  when numerator is used without denominator, denominator is the smallest power of 10 greater than the numerator.
  examples:
    1p = 1
    1p2n = 1.2
    1p2n3d = 1.66
    2n3d = 0.66
    3d = 0.33
    1p3d = 1.33
    
Thickness specifiers:
  T = frame and big details and little details
  F = frame
  D = big details and little details
  B = big details
  L = little details
